    Buttons

    • KEY1Sleep / wake — tap to put the frame to sleep, tap again to wake it.
    • KEY2Refresh — re‑fetch and redraw the current artwork.
    • KEY3Wake — wakes the frame from sleep.
    • KEY1 + KEY3Factory reset — hold both together for ~15 s to wipe Wi‑Fi & pairing and return to onboarding.

    Forgetting unpairs this frame from your account. It keeps its Wi‑Fi and shows the pairing screen, ready to re‑add.

    Full wipe: clears Wi‑Fi and pairing on the frame and returns it to onboarding from scratch — for re‑gifting or moving networks. The frame must be online.
