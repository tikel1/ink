Connect a frame
  1. Pairing mode. A new frame is ready on first power‑on. To re‑pair one, hold KEY3 ~5 s — the screen shows “Pairing mode”.
  2. Join the frame's Wi‑Fi. In your phone's Wi‑Fi settings, join “Ink Frame” (or scan the QR on the screen). A setup page opens on its own.
  3. Pick your home Wi‑Fi and enter the password. The frame connects, then shows a pairing QR + 6‑digit code.
  4. Pair. Scan that QR, or type the code below.
